Air Conditioning Services

Keep Your Aircon Running Efficiently and Clean
At HandyHome, we specialize in aircon cleaning services to ensure your system runs efficiently, reduces energy consumption, and provides you with fresh, cool air. Regular maintenance is key to prolonging the life of your aircon and improving air quality in your home.

What We Offer

  • Full System Cleaning — Comprehensive cleaning of your aircon’s internal components for better performance.
  • Filter Replacement & Cleaning — Replace or clean filters to ensure maximum airflow and energy efficiency.
  • Coil Cleaning — Clean the evaporator and condenser coils to prevent blockages and maintain cooling efficiency.
  • Drainage Cleaning — Clear any clogs in the drainage lines to avoid water leakage and further damage to your aircon.
  • Freon Level Check — Ensure the refrigerant is at the correct level to optimize cooling performance.
  • Preventive Maintenance — Regular cleaning to prevent future breakdowns and improve the lifespan of your aircon.
