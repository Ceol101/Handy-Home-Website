Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  1. How do I book a service?
    Simply go to the “Services” tab, choose the type of service you need (Plumbing, Electrical, Air-condition Cleaning or General Repairs), and click “Book Now.”
  2. What areas do you serve?
    Our office is located at Paseo Arcenas, Banawa, Cebu City. We primarily serve clients in talamban, Apas, and Bacayan areas, with plans to expand soon!
  3. How do I pay for my booking?
    We accept GCash, debit/credit cards, and cash-on-service. For subscribers, monthly fees are automatically billed based on your plan.
  4. What if my service provider doesn’t arrive on time?
    You can contact our Support Team through chat or phone. We’ll immediately coordinate with your assigned professional and resolve the issue.
  5. Can I cancel or reschedule my booking?
    Yes! Go to your account → My Bookings → Reschedule or Cancel. No fees apply if you cancel 12 hours before your appointment.
