About HandyHome

HandyHome is your trusted partner for convenient and reliable home maintenance solutions. We provide professional services such as plumbing, electrical, cleaning, and handyman work — all in one easy-to-use platform.

Our Mission

To make home maintenance simple, accessible, and affordable for every Filipino household. We believe your home deserves expert care, and we’re here to deliver quality service with a smile.

Our Vision

To be the leading home service provider in the Philippines by bringing skilled professionals and homeowners together through modern technology and trustworthy service.

What We Offer

  • Professional home maintenance services
  • Affordable subscription plans
  • Fast and easy online booking
  • Trained and verified technicians

At HandyHome, your comfort is our top priority. From quick fixes to regular maintenance, we’re ready to serve — anytime, anywhere.

Why Choose HandyHome?
